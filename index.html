How Much is Enough?

This visualization presents a global overview of living costs, with a focus on identifying cities that offer economic advantages. By analyzing average net salaries in various locations, it offers valuable insights for individuals, businesses, and researchers. This user-friendly tool equips the audience with data-driven information to make informed decisions regarding their economic interests and opportunities.

A Peek of Living cost through McMeal Price

Although the price of a McDonald's meal is not a perfect measure of living costs,
it can serve as a quick and simple way to make rough comparisons between locations.
It's an accessible and relatable indicator that can provide a general sense of the cost of living in different places, especially when considered alongside other economic factors.
